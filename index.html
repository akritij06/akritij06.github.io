About

I am currently a Research Associate at Adobe Research, where I work on large language models, retrieval-augmented generation, and AI-assisted decision making.

I graduated in 2024 with a B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering from IIT Roorkee. My broader interests span multimodal content generation, LLM optimization, and information reliability in AI systems.

Research Interests

  • Large Language Models (LLMs) and retrieval-augmented generation
  • Decision-support and multi-document reasoning
  • Input-adaptive LLM optimization and compression
  • Uncertainty estimation & reliability in AI systems
  • Multimodal content generation
